Feline Fishing Frenzy



    Instructions

  • Welcome to Feline Fishing Frenzy!
  • Your goal is to catch as many fish as possible.
  • Use your mouse to move your line up and down to catch fish. Click when above water to "reel in" the fish.
  • But watch out for obstacles! Sharks will eat your bait, and jellyfish will shock your line.
  • Pufferfish and turtles will knock your fish off the hook.
  • Look out for extra bait to collect in the water!
